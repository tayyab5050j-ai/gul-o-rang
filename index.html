Our Story

Every Twist Tells a Story

Twist & Bloom was born from a simple belief: that beautiful things can be made from the most unexpected materials. What started as a rainy afternoon craft became a passion — transforming humble pipe cleaners into intricate, lifelike flowers that never fade.

Each flower is hand-twisted, petal by petal, using premium chenille stems. No two are exactly alike, because the beauty of handmade is in the imperfection. Every bloom carries the time, care, and intention of the hands that shaped it.

We source our materials in small batches and take pride in a process that asks for nothing but patience and creativity. Whether you're decorating your home or looking for a gift that lasts, each flower carries a little piece of that original wonder.
