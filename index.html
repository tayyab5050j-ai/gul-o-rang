About Us

Every Twist Tells a Story

We turn humble pipe cleaners into everlasting blooms, one petal at a time.

gul-o-rang was born from a simple belief: beautiful things can come from the most unexpected materials. What started as a quiet afternoon craft became a passion — transforming humble pipe cleaners into flowers that never fade.

Each petal is hand-twisted using premium chenille stems. No two blooms are the same, because the beauty of handmade lies in the imperfection. Every flower carries the time and care of the hands that shaped it.

Whether you're brightening your home or gifting something that lasts, each piece carries a little piece of that original wonder.
